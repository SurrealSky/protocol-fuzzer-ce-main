== Peach 3 Pit Files ==

Peach Pit files are XML files that contain all of the information needed for Peach to perform a fuzzing run.  When you fuzz something with Peach you will be creating a Peach Pit file.

Peach Pit files contain the following:

 ** link:GeneralConfiguration.html[General Configuration]
 ** link:DataModel.html[Data Modeling]
 ** link:StateModel.html[State Modeling]
 ** link:AgentsMonitors.html[Agents and Monitors]
 ** link:TestConfig.html[Test Configuration]

You might also want to know how to link:DebuggingPitFiles.html[debug pit files] and how you can link:ValidatingPitFiles.html[validate your pit files] as well.
